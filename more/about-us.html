Keribu.ug(Keribu Uganda) was designed by travon Incorporated in 2014 to help ease selling and buying of rooms of accommodation in Uganda. We develop websites for our clients with special business needs. We also develop mobile(Both Android and Windows Mobile Software) and desktop application.

Our network provides resources and tools for consumers and sellers to help navigate all stages of the buying cycle. These tools give consumers access to the most comprehensive selection of existing rooms for sale, hostel locations and more. It also has the deepest consumer engagement among hostel and rentals' websites – consumers spend more time on our websites and mobile experiences

travon Incorporated was started by Aguma Chales Jalameso, a professional web developer with a Bachelors Degree in Software Engineering from Makerere University, as an effect of uganda's lacking of technological intergration into its societies.

This was invented to help those paying visits to their friends to locate the hostels in which their friends do reside.

  • Go to home page enter any hostel name in the search box provided and click the search button to enable the transaction.

  • If not certain of the hostel's name, please enter a clue that can enable us give you a list collection of hostels corresponding to your clue.

  • Incase you fail to see the hostel you are looking for, please fill the form that will displayed to you, and give the details you probably know about and we'll take a look into your request as soon as possible because our aim is satisfy our customers' needs in the best way possible.
